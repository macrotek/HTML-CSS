Dogs Living

posted by Viraj Desai on 17th july, 2021

One of the main defects of the Indian agricultural marketing is the presence of too many middleman and exploitation of farmers by them. On one hand these middlemen exploit the farmers by purchasing the produce at lower prices and on the other hand they exploit the customers by demanding higher prices from them. The only aim of number of commission agents, brokers etc. is to derive a higher income from the middle processes. These middlemen take undue advantage of the poor farmer on the basis of their financial resources.
