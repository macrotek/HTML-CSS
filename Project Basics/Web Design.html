This project is an application-based shopping system for an existing shop.
The project objective is to deliver the online shopping application into android platform.
Online shopping is the process whereby consumers directly buy vegetables or services from a seller in real-time, without an intermediary service, over the Internet.
It is a form of electronic commerce.
This project is an attempt to provide the advantages of online vegetable shopping to customers of a real market.
It helps buying the products in the shop anywhere through internet by using an android device.
Thus, the customer will get the service of online vegetable shopping and home delivery as per his requirements
